Disclaimer & Terms of Use

Rate accuracy

Exchange rates and fees shown on Coinnect are sourced from provider APIs and published pricing pages. Rates update approximately every 3 minutes for live sources. For providers without a public API, fees are based on published pricing and marked with "~est." — these are estimates and may not reflect the exact rate you will receive.

No liability

Coinnect is an information service only. We do not execute transfers, hold funds, or act as a financial intermediary. We make no warranty, express or implied, about the accuracy, completeness, or timeliness of the rates displayed. Any financial decision made based on information shown on this site is at your sole risk. Coinnect and its operators accept no responsibility or liability for any loss or damage arising from reliance on this information.

Always verify

Before executing any transfer, verify the current rate and fees directly with the provider on their official website or app. Rates can change in seconds, and providers may apply different fees based on your account type, payment method, or jurisdiction.

Analytics & cookies

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand aggregate usage patterns. No personal financial data is transmitted to GA4. You can opt out at any time by visiting coinnect.bot/?notrack or by clicking "Decline analytics" on the cookie banner.

No affiliation

Coinnect is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any of the providers listed. Provider names and logos are trademarks of their respective owners.
